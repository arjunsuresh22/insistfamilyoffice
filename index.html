Insist Family Office is a private investment vehicle dedicated to backing exceptional founders building transformative businesses across emerging and frontier categories.

We take a long-term view, partnering with companies where technology and purpose intersect — from intelligent consumer products to sustainable living and the future of mobility.

Our approach

Patient capital.
Active partnership.

At Insist Family Office, we believe that lasting value is created at the intersection of bold vision and disciplined execution. We invest in founders who are uncompromising in their ambition and purposeful in how they build.

Our focus spans consumer technology, robotics & automation, sustainable products, and financial inclusion — sectors we believe are at the leading edge of structural, generational change in India and beyond.

Beyond capital, we bring a network of relationships, operational experience, and strategic guidance to help our portfolio companies navigate critical inflection points.
