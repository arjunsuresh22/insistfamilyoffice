Insist Family Office is the private investment vehicle of R Suresh and family. R Suresh is a pioneer in Executive Search and Leadership Consulting in India, with over 30 years of experience building category-defining firms — including Stanton Chase India (acquired by Recruit Holdings, Japan), Recruit India, and now Insist Consulting.

Arjun Suresh, son of R Suresh, brings a decade of product development experience across drones, robotics, and autonomous vehicles. He has served as Product Manager at Shield AI (backed by Andreessen Horowitz) and Uber ATG (now Aurora), and holds a Master's degree from Carnegie Mellon University.

The family office invests across listed equity, private equity, and early stage companies — with a focus on robotics, AI, healthcare and sustainability.
